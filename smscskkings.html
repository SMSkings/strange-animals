Strange Animals

There are many strange animals on earth, but what can we learn from these odd creatures? This essay shows just a few of the strangest animals on our earth.

Right off the bat, we have the peculiar Axolotl, pronounced (ACK-SHU-LOH-TULL). Axolotls are fascinating creatures that can live up to 15 years in the wild.

Axolotl - Ambystoma mexicanum

Right off the bat, we have the peculiar Axolotl. Axolotl is one of the strange animals and it is pronounced (ACK-SHU-LOH-TULL) and it is a Mexican Salamander. It weighs 2.1 to 8 ounces. It can live up to 15 years in the wild. It’s main habitat is in water but it can go on land. It has feathery fins behind it’s head and it has a range of possible colors, such as white, black, pink, yellow, brown, and even blue. They mature about 18 to 24 months into their lives. They can be 12 inches long. The Axolotl is a curious creature.

Aye-Aye - Daubentonia madagascariensis

The second animal is the aye-aye, a weird and uncanny creature. The Aye-Aye is an elusive, nocturnal primate that lives in Madagascar. Its diet includes grubs and fruit. To find the grubs, it uses its long middle finger to tap at wood, and so it can find its next meal. It also likes coconuts, and other fruits. The Aye-Aye is 4-7 pounds and it is 12-16 inches long. It has large eyes and sensitive ears, so it can see and hear at night, when it is awake. There is not much known about this elusive creature, but scientists are working to find out more. Part of the reason not much is known is that it is an endangered species, meaning that there are not many of them left because of hunting or just because its natural predators have upped their game and more Aye-Ayes are being hunted. One of these natural predators is the Fossa. The Fossa is a carnivore that looks like a dog, but it can climb trees, making it harder for it’s prey to escape, especially with its main food source being lemurs and monkeys.

Blobfish - Psychrolutes marcidus

The third animal we are listing is the bizarre blobfish. Have you ever heard of it? It is a strange ocean-dwelling creature that is the ugliest animal in the world. It is a pale, pink gelatinous blob with a droopy downturned mouth and a long, sagging nose. It is about a foot long and it is an omnivore. It is almost unrecognizable underwater. This tadpole-shaped fish has a bulbous head, a large mouth, tapered tails, and feathered pectoral fins. However, according to scientist Richard Arnott, this visual image of the blobfish is nothing more than a “Vast bullying campaign”. The blobfish is a weird creature.

Goblin shark - Mitsukurina owstoni

The fourth creature on the list is the unusual goblin shark. The goblin shark is an elusive, slow moving, deep living shark. Fun fact: it lives up to 1,800 feet below sea level! It preys mostly on small fish, squid, shrimps, and other soft-bodied creatures. It has small eyes and pinkish, whitish skin. It has a maximum length of 14 feet long. It has dagger-like front teeth. (Because this shark lives so deep down, and it moves so slow, it is not a threat to humans.) It lives in the west Atlantic, near South Africa, the western Indian Ocean, and near Japan and Australia. The goblin shark is a strange creature.

Glass frog - Centrolenidae

The fifth creature on this list is the bizarre glass frog. The strangest thing about these creatures is that they are see-through! They are classified in approximately 160 species and 12 genera. They are found in tropical forests to mid-elevation mountain forests. Most species are small, with adults ranging from 20 to 30 millimeters (0.8 to 1.2 inches) in total length. Some species, however, reach a maximum length of 80mm, or 3 inches. The glass frog is a strange creature.

Pink fairy armadillo - Chlamyphorus truncatus

The last animal on the list is the odd pink fairy armadillo. The pink fairy armadillo is a small, elusive mammal that is good at digging and is endangered. It has large claws and has armor plates around part of its tail and the entire body and head. The armor plates are whitish and yellowish brown and its fur is whitish. The population of this creature is unknown. It eats insects, larvae, snails, worms, plant material, small reptiles such as lizards and snakes, and bird eggs. Its head and body are 5.5-7 inches long and its tail is 1.5 inches long. It is nocturnal and lives in Paraguay, Western and Central Bolivia, and extreme Northern Argentina. The pink fairy armadillo is a strange creature.

Conclusion

In conclusion, strange animals are a true wonder to our world and the axolotl, aye-aye, blobfish, goblin shark, glass frog, and pink fairy armadillo are just a few of them. Have you ever seen these weird creatures before?
